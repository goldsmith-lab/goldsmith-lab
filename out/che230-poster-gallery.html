Chemical Engineering in Everyday Life

Poster Project Gallery @ Michigan

Many people are not aware of the large impact chemical engineering has on our everyday lives. To help change this, UofM sophomore chemical engineering students are tasked with creating posters about chemical engineering in everyday life to share with the community and each other. This project culminates in an outreach event where the students present their posters to colleagues and to grade schoolers from Detroit.

Check out some of the posters below to learn more about what chemical engineers do!

Interested in learning more about chemical engineering equipment that you have shown in the posters? Check out the Visual Encyclopedia of Chemical Engineering.
